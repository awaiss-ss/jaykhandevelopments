Proposed Design by JKD

Alkhidmat Centre, F-6 Markaz — Islamabad

Submitted to Capital Development Authority (CDA) for the establishment of Alkhidmat Centre at F-6 Markaz, Islamabad

A contemporary civic architecture concept envisioned for the proposed Alkhidmat Centre in F-6, Islamabad. The design reflects dignity, transparency, and service to the community through a harmonious blend of modern architecture, natural materials, and Islamic design heritage.

The concept incorporates premium natural stone cladding, warm timber finishes, expansive glazed façades, landscaped public spaces, and elegant lighting to create a welcoming environment for visitors. A feature Islamic geometric screen (Mashrabiya) serves as both an architectural statement and a functional shading element, celebrating cultural identity while enhancing environmental performance.

Designed with functionality and longevity in mind, the proposal emphasizes sustainable materials, efficient circulation, abundant natural light, and a timeless architectural language suited to a public institution dedicated to community welfare.

A timeless facade that reflects dignity, trust and service through the use of natural materials, greenery, Islamic patterns and modern architecture.

About Us

Jaykhan Developments (Private) Limited

Crafting Timeless Real Estate & Infrastructure

Jaykhan Developments (Private) Limited is an Islamabad, Pakistan-based real estate development and turnkey construction firm committed to delivering exceptional residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects. We combine architectural excellence, engineering precision, and meticulous execution to create developments that stand the test of time.

With expertise spanning project planning, design management, construction, and development, we provide comprehensive end-to-end solutions from concept to completion. Every project reflects our commitment to quality, innovation, functionality, and enduring value.

Our portfolio includes completed projects across the residential, commercial, and infrastructure sectors, delivered for private clients as well as government and semi-government organizations. We also have experience working alongside corporate clients and humanitarian organizations on specialized construction and development assignments.
